Bus Game Rules 🚌

The game begins with a shuffled standard 52-card deck. The player must advance through four stages.
In each stage, a prediction is made, and a card is revealed from the top of the deck to determine the outcome.

1. Red or Black

The player predicts the color (Red or Black) of the next card. The card is drawn and revealed.
If the guess matches the card's color, the game proceeds.

2. Higher or Lower

Based on the first card revealed, the player predicts if the second card will have a higher or lower value.
The second card is drawn and compared to the first.

3. Between or Outside

The values of the first two cards create a numeric range.
The player predicts if the third card will fall strictly inside or outside these two numbers.
The third card is drawn and revealed.

4. Guess the Suit

For the final card, the player must predict the specific suit (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, or Spades).
The fourth card is drawn to verify the prediction.


i. Deck Reset

Cards are dealt continuously from the deck.
If the deck is exhausted during gameplay, it is automatically reshuffled to continue the game.

ii. Counter

The counter placed at the bottom of the table displays how many times the player guessed wrong.
If that number exceeds 20 tries, a visual effect appears on the screen representing the tiredness of the player. Drink up!
